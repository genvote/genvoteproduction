OUR PROGRAMS

JUST DEMOCRACY FOR ALL (JDA) ORGANIZING FELLOWSHIP

The GenVote National Just Democracy for All (JDA) Organizing Fellows program is a paid opportunity for 15 working-class fellows, ages 15-35, to develop as leaders in the GenVote network with 1-1 coaching, training to spark new GenVote Teams and Hubs, and inspire hundreds of young people to fight for our right to vote and a just democracy for all.

learn more



GENVOTE ASCENDERS

We are looking for a cohort of 5 organizers in Montana to join GenVote Ascenders! Ascenders is the first and only program in the United States to mobilize young people around critical Secretary of State elections. This is a paid opportunity for a cohort of highly motivated young people to mobilize young voters, gaining high-level experience in leadership development, communications, community organizing, and electoral strategy (including phone/text banking, and voter registration).

learn more



GENVOTE DEFENDERS

It’s going to be up to our generation to restore trust in our democracy and defend our freedom to vote in safe and fair elections. That’s why we’re launching GenVote Defenders - the only nonpartisan national youth-led program mobilizing Gen-Z to recruit hundreds of young people to become election protection volunteers, help voters exercise their right to vote, and mobilize young people to defend against potential attempts of election subversion in local communities across the country.

learn more



SAVE OUR GENERATION'S DEMOCRACY PLEDGE

If you believe that the people who run our elections should use their power to protect our right to vote, stop voter suppression and election subversion, and to expand voting rights for future generations, send them the pledge today! For elected officials, you can show your commitment to save our generation's democracy and fight for our right to vote by signing our Save Our Generation's Democracy Pledge.
