GenVote 2021 - 22 Impact Report

Read the GenVote 2021 - 2022 Impact Report now!


The GenVote 2021 - 2022 Impact Report summarizes a transformative two years for GenVote. We underwent an intense frontloading process and relaunched our organization with a new theory of change and movement structure, pulled off intensive direct actions across the country, and passed legislation to make voting more accessible for young people in New York. We even trained dozens of GenVoters to protect and get out the youth vote and launched a historic youth-led campaign to defeat election deniers and insurrectionists running for Secretaries of State in critical states in the 2022 midterm elections. We hope our 2021 - 2022 Impact Report will serve as a reminder of our accomplishments throughout our frontloading journey and inspire you to join our movement to make electoral justice a major priority for our generation.
