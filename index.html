WE ARE SPARKING THE

PRO-DEMOCRACY

MOVEMENT FOR OUR GENERATION

Let’s be real: Our generation is facing a democratic crisis. Across the country, politicians are passing anti-voter laws to silence young people, targeting election workers, and rigging maps to lock in power for decades.This isn’t just voter suppression—it’s a billionaire-backed movement to burn democracy to the ground at every level of government.


But like a phoenix, GenVoters are rising to meet the moment and building the pro-democracy movement that our generation deserves.

We’ve grown into a national, youth-led movement grounded in the leadership of everyday young people who believe a truly just, multiracial democracy is worth fighting for. We’re building political homes via our GenVote Teams and Hubs, organizing local electoral justice campaigns, and fighting for a multiracial democracy that works for all of us, not just billionaires and a minority few.

WHAT WE DO

We’re sparking a movement of young people fighting for our right to vote and a just democracy for all
Generation Vote is a national movement home where young people are trained and developed as leaders fighting for a just democracy in their communities through creative actions and legislative campaigns, advance innovative solutions to expand youth voting rights and civic engagement, and build pro-democracy coalitions across movements.


WHO WE ARE


We are a cross-class, multiracial movement of young people tearing down the barriers that prevent young people from participating in our democracy. Since 2022, we’ve expanded our movement to over ten states and Washington D.C., establishing ourselves as a leading force in the fight for a just democracy for all. Are you ready to join us?
