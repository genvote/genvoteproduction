GenVote @

We believe in the power of student-driven, bottom-up organizing. To have our voices heard, we need to expand our scope of political engagement beyond the voting booth. For students, Generation Vote expands the scope of political engagement through our unique model of civic engagement. For candidates, our GenVote teams provide the necessary resources to run innovative youth-focused campaigns in their local communities.

To elevate our voices in local politics, we believe that we need to be in positions to not just participate in the political process but to lead it.

We provide the educational tools that empower GenVote team members with the skills that you need to pioneer research efforts, develop policy platforms, and lead campaigns.
