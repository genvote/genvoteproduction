OUR GENERATION'S ELECTORAL JUSTICE SUMMIT







THE 1ST YOUTH LED ELECTORAL JUSTICE SUMMIT IN THE UNITED STATES

June 21-24, 2024

Phoenix, AZ


Join us for “Our Generation’s Electoral Justice Summit,” the first youth-led, cross-movement electoral justice summit in the United States.

In June 2024, GenVote will hold the first cross-movement, national youth-led electoral justice gathering to reimagine how future generations come of age in our democracy and enact a unified agenda for electoral justice that can expand the franchise for generations to come. We will bring together 100-120 leaders to determine how we can make electoral justice a major issue in the 2024 election cycle and prepare a historic push for major democracy wins at the federal level in January 2025 and beyond.

What we hope to accomplish together in this space:

  • Convene the first youth-led space to reflect on the state of our movements, level-set [youth] voter suppression challenges and discuss state-of-play for federal democracy reform in 2024 and 2025
  • Strategize how the youth movement can prepare for and fight against fascist attempts to take over our elections & democratic institutions at the federal, state and local level
  • Build relationships with partners and create trusted containers for collaboration on electoral justice fights in our local communities
  • Popularize the concept of “electoral justice” and lay the foundation for a united electoral justice platform that can transform our democracy and expand the franchise for generations to come.

To attend the Summit, you must be affiliated with an invited organization or receive an invitation as an individual. All invitees must register and the registration deadline is May 17, 2024.

We hope you will be able to join us at this historic gathering!

Our invite-only convening will focus on bringing together 100-120 people from values-aligned youth-led movement organizations, democracy organizations, and BIPOC-led social movements that are not traditionally represented in the established democracy ecosystem to attend the gathering. Each invited organization can bring 1-3 people.
Phoenix Renaissance Downtown Hotel (in-person only)
GenVote is doing our best to make the Summit as accessible for organizations and advocates as possible. With this in mind - and with the support of a few organizations providing financial assistance - we have created a registration fee of $200 to help recoup some of the costs of food and convening space. If you are coming from an organization or personal background with resources, we ask that you pay what you can so we can support participants and organizations from varying economic backgrounds:

$200 per person - Full registration fee (for attendees coming from orgs with budgets of 500K or higher)
$125 per person- Subsidized fee (for applicants applying as individuals with annual incomes under $40,000, or who are coming from organizations with budgets under $500k)

Limited # of travel scholarships and full registration fee waivers will be evaluated as needed! Travel is not included in this cost and are asking organizations/individuals to cover your own travel to Phoenix, however we will consider applications for travel scholarship and registration fee waivers as needed.

GenVote is committed to hosting accessible, inclusive events. We will provide accessible accommodations and language interpretation, if needed.

If you have any general questions, please contact Brianna Cea at bcea@genvote.org. If you have specific logistics questions, please contact Brendan Cushing at bcushing@genvote.org.
