Take Action

Our current model of political engagement is outdated and one-sided. In this political moment, it is easy to lose faith in a political system that does not reflect our values. Too often we are asked for our time, money, and energy, without anything in return. In order to unleash the power of our generation, we need to change the narrative and turn the political system upside down. We can no longer wait on the sidelines.

To change the narrative, we will launch GenVotes teams in communities across the country, with a focus on communities with high potential for youth engagement.But in order for this to happen, we need your help. If you are a student or faculty member at a University, take action today and learn how you can bring a GenVote program to your campus. If you are a candidate running for office, learn more about our resources for candidates and GenVote partnerships below.
