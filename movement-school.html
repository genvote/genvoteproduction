MOVEMENT SCHOOL


GenVote is sparking our movement by starting GenVote Teams and Hubs in colleges, high schools, places of faith, rural counties and big cities. GenVote Teams and Hubs are open to college students, high schoolers, post-graduates, and any allies aligned with our DNA. Learn more about GenVote Teams and Hubs here.

Our Movement School will recruit young people of all ages to launch local, creative actions that will create an active, large base of advocates fighting for electoral justice in their communities. Our trainings will allow anyone to get involved in counties, cities and campuses across the country.
